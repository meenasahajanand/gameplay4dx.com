Description

Super Stack is a fun and challenging puzzle game in which you have to stack every given shape without losing any of them. Stacking squares is a simple task for everybody, but it could get a little tricky once you add triangles and circles to it. Try to find a way to stack them up and have them all safe and balanced.

The games starts with simple squares which are rather easy to stack but wait until there are triangles, circles and or bars for you to stack in between the squares. Do you think you can stack all those forms without anything falling down? Find out now and have fun with Super Stack.
